SHOW SOME LOVE

Since retiring from tennis, 2013 Wimbledon Champion Marion Bartoli has been devoted to bringing
high-fashion to the court. Her latest project, a partnership with Fila entitled LOVE FILA,infuses the
beloved athletic mainstay with the vibrant colors and bold graphics that have been Marion'ssignature
since her tennis days. The result is a one-of-a-kind collection suited to athletes and fashionistas alike.

In an effort to balance Fila's forward thinking brand ethos with Marions creative vibrance DOMANI
launched LOVE FILA,a platform celebrating the color,attitude,and energy at the center of Marion's
latest line.

smooth visual experience.
